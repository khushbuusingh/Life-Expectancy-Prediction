Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the purpose of this website?

This website provides insights and tools for predicting life expectancy based on personal health data.

2. How does the life expectancy calculator work?

The calculator asks you a series of health-related questions and uses statistical models to provide an estimate of your life expectancy.

3. Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously and follow best practices to ensure your information is kept private.

4. Can I trust the predictions provided?

While we strive for accuracy, predictions are estimates based on statistical averages and should not be considered definitive.

5. How can I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team through the Contact Us page or via email at support@example.com.

6. Are there any costs associated with using the calculator?

No, the life expectancy calculator is free to use for all users.

7. Can I change my answers after submitting?

Yes, you can go back and edit your answers at any time to get updated predictions.
